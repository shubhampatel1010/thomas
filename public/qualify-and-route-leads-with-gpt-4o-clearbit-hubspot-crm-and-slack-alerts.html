What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical challenge of efficiently qualifying and distributing incoming leads. Traditional manual qualification processes are slow, inconsistent, and often miss important signals in lead data. The workflow combines AI intelligence with CRM automation to create a seamless lead management system.

When a new lead submits your website form, the system automatically evaluates their potential using GPT-4o's natural language processing combined with Clearbit's firmographic data. High-quality leads are immediately routed to the appropriate sales rep in HubSpot CRM while your team gets instant Slack notifications about hot prospects that require urgent follow-up.

How It Works

1. Lead Capture

The workflow begins when a webhook receives form submissions from your website. This triggers the automation process instantly as soon as a new lead enters your system.

2. Data Enrichment

Clearbit automatically appends valuable firmographic data to the lead record, including company size, industry, technology stack, and other business intelligence that helps assess lead quality.

3. AI Qualification

GPT-4o analyzes the lead's form responses and enriched data using your predefined scoring criteria. The AI evaluates factors like budget, authority, need, and timeline to generate an accurate lead score.

4. Intelligent Routing

Based on the lead score and other attributes, the system automatically assigns the lead to the most appropriate sales rep in HubSpot CRM. Routing rules can consider territory, product interest, or rep specialization.

5. Instant Alerts

High-priority leads trigger immediate Slack notifications to sales reps, ensuring rapid response to your hottest prospects. The alerts include key details to enable personalized outreach.
