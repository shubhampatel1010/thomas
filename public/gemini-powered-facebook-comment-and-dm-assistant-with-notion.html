What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of managing high volumes of customer inquiries on Facebook pages and Messenger. Businesses often struggle to respond quickly to repetitive questions about products, pricing, or policies, leading to frustrated customers and missed sales opportunities.

The workflow uses Gemini AI to analyze incoming comments and direct messages, generate accurate responses based on your knowledge base, and automatically log the entire interaction in Notion. This creates a seamless customer experience while providing your team with complete visibility into all social media interactions.

How It Works

1. Monitoring Facebook Interactions

The system continuously monitors your Facebook page for new comments and direct messages. It captures the complete message content along with customer profile information and timestamps.

2. AI-Powered Response Generation

Gemini AI processes each inquiry, understanding the intent behind customer questions. It references your product information and FAQ knowledge base to generate accurate, brand-appropriate responses tailored to each query.

3. Automated Response Delivery

The system posts replies directly to comments or sends Messenger responses while maintaining natural conversation flow. For complex inquiries beyond its configured thresholds, it flags these for human follow-up.

4. Notion Integration

Every interaction is automatically logged in your Notion database with complete context - original message, AI response, timestamps, and customer details. This creates a searchable record for customer service and analytics.
