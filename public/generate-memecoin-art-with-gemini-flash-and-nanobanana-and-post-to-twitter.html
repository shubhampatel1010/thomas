How It Works

1. Trend Analysis

The system first scans current meme trends and cryptocurrency discussions to identify relevant themes. It analyzes what types of visual content are performing best across social platforms.

2. AI Art Generation

Gemini Flash creates base artwork combining crypto elements with trending visual styles. The AI references successful past posts from your account to maintain brand consistency.

3. Meme Customization

NanoBanana then applies meme-specific enhancements like captions, filters, and formatting tweaks that increase shareability. The system can generate multiple variations for A/B testing.

4. Approval & Scheduling

Before posting, the workflow can optionally send creations for human review. Approved memes are automatically scheduled based on your account's historical engagement data.
