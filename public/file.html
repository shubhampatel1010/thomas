What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the time-consuming and error-prone process of manually creating and sending appointment letters to new hires. It combines Google Docs for professional templates, an HR approval step for quality control, and automated email delivery through Gmail - all while maintaining a complete audit trail of the process.

Traditional hiring processes often involve copying templates, manually filling in details, chasing approvals, and keeping track of sent documents. This workflow eliminates those inefficiencies while ensuring every candidate receives a perfectly formatted, approved appointment letter exactly when they should.

How It Works

1. Document Generation from Template

The workflow starts by pulling candidate information from your HR system or spreadsheet. It then creates a new Google Doc by merging this data with your pre-approved appointment letter template, ensuring consistent formatting and content.

2. HR Approval Workflow

Newly generated documents are automatically sent to designated HR approvers via email with a direct link to review. Approvers can check details and either approve, request changes, or reject the document - all without leaving their inbox.

3. Secure Document Delivery

Once approved, the system automatically sends the final appointment letter to the candidate via Gmail. You can configure CCs to relevant parties and track when the document has been opened by the recipient.
