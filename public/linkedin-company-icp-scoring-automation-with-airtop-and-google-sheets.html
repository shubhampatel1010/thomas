What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of manually evaluating hundreds of LinkedIn company profiles to identify your best potential customers. By automatically scoring companies against your ideal customer profile (ICP) criteria, your sales team can focus outreach on prospects with the highest conversion potential.

The workflow extracts key signals from LinkedIn company pages - including employee growth, tech stack mentions, funding status, and hiring trends - then applies your custom scoring model in Google Sheets via Airtop. Companies are ranked based on their fit, with color-coded visualizations highlighting your hottest leads.

How It Works

Step 1: LinkedIn Data Extraction

The workflow starts by pulling structured data from LinkedIn company profiles, including firmographics, growth metrics, and technology indicators that correlate with buying intent in your market.

Step 2: Google Sheets Integration

Company data flows into a centralized Google Sheet where your ICP scoring model lives. The sheet maintains historical data to track how companies' scores change over time.

Step 3: Airtop Scoring Engine

Airtop applies your weighted scoring rules to each company, calculating an overall ICP match score. You can adjust weights for different attributes (e.g., 2x points for using a competing product).

Step 4: Prioritization Dashboard

The final output is a prioritized list of companies with their complete scoring breakdown, enabling your team to tailor outreach based on each prospect's specific strengths as an ICP match.

Pro tip: Create separate scoring models for different product lines or customer segments. The workflow can run multiple scoring algorithms in parallel.
