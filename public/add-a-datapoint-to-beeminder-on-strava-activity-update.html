What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of manually tracking fitness progress across multiple apps. When you complete a workout recorded in Strava (like a run, ride, or swim), the system automatically adds that activity as a datapoint to your Beeminder goal. This creates a seamless connection between your actual workouts and your progress tracking.

The workflow transforms Strava activity data into Beeminder-compatible metrics, whether you're tracking distance, duration, frequency, or other measurable aspects of your fitness routine. By automating this process, you eliminate the friction of manual logging while gaining the motivational benefits of Beeminder's commitment contracts and progress visualizations.

How It Works

1. Strava Activity Trigger

The workflow monitors your Strava account for new activities. Whenever you complete a workout and it appears in your Strava feed, the system detects this update immediately.

2. Data Processing

The workflow extracts relevant metrics from your Strava activity based on your goal type. For distance goals, it might pull mileage; for duration goals, it captures time spent; for frequency goals, it counts the activity itself.

3. Beeminder Integration

Processed data gets formatted into Beeminder's required structure and posted to your specific goal. The system handles authentication and ensures the datapoint appears correctly in your Beeminder graph.
