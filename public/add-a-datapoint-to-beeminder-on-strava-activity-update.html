What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the problem of inconsistent fitness tracking between apps. Many athletes use Strava to record workouts but then manually transfer data to Beeminder for goal tracking - a process that's easily forgotten and creates gaps in progress monitoring.

The workflow automatically bridges this gap by detecting new Strava activities and converting them into Beeminder datapoints. Whether you're tracking running mileage, cycling distance, or workout frequency, this integration ensures your Beeminder goals always reflect your actual activity.

How It Works

1. Strava Activity Detection

The workflow continuously monitors your Strava account for new activities. When you complete a run, ride, or other tracked workout, Strava sends the activity data to the workflow.

2. Data Processing

The system extracts relevant metrics from the Strava activity based on your Beeminder goal parameters. This could include distance, duration, elevation gain, or simply activity completion.

3. Beeminder Integration

Processed data is formatted into a Beeminder-compatible datapoint and sent to your specified goal. The update happens typically within minutes of activity completion.
