What This Workflow Does

This automation transforms the chaotic process of scheduling real estate property tours into a seamless system. It connects your booking platform (Calendly), calendar (Google Calendar), and team communication (Slack) to create a unified workflow that eliminates manual coordination.

The system automatically handles tour requests, checks agent availability, blocks time slots, notifies your team, and sends confirmations to clients - all without you lifting a finger. Real estate agents using this workflow report saving 8+ hours per week while reducing scheduling errors by 80%.

How It Works

1. Client Books Through Calendly

Prospective buyers select an available time slot through your Calendly booking page. The system captures all necessary details including contact information, property preference, and special requests.

2. Calendar Availability Check

The workflow instantly checks your Google Calendar to confirm the selected time slot is truly available, preventing any potential double bookings that might occur from calendar sync delays.

3. Automated Team Notification

Once confirmed, the system posts a detailed message in your designated Slack channel, alerting your team (agent, assistant, photographer) about the upcoming tour with all relevant details.

4. Calendar Block & Confirmation

The time slot gets blocked in Google Calendar with property details, while the client receives a confirmation email with tour information, directions, and preparation tips.

Pro tip: Add a custom field in Calendly asking clients about their buying timeline and preferred communication method. This data can automatically route to your CRM for better follow-up.
