What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the critical problem of lost leads and delayed follow-up in manual intake processes. When potential customers submit your Tally form, the workflow instantly captures their information in your Google Sheets CRM, categorizes the lead based on form responses, and sends a personalized email through SendGrid - all without any manual intervention.

The system eliminates spreadsheet copy-paste, ensures data consistency, and creates immediate touchpoints that dramatically improve conversion rates. It transforms your form from a passive data collector into an active sales enablement tool that works 24/7.

How It Works

1. Tally Form Submission Trigger

The workflow activates whenever someone submits your Tally form. It captures all form fields including custom questions, file uploads, and conditional responses.

2. Data Validation & Enrichment

The system checks for missing required fields, formats data consistently (like phone numbers), and adds metadata like timestamp, lead source, and geographic tags based on IP address.

3. Google Sheets CRM Update

The lead data gets appended to your designated Google Sheet with proper column mapping. The workflow can create new tabs for different lead categories or update existing records if the email matches.

4. SendGrid Email Automation

Based on form responses, the system triggers tailored email sequences through SendGrid. These can include attachments, dynamic content blocks, and links to schedule calls or make payments.

Pro tip: Add a hidden field in your Tally form to track campaign sources. This lets you measure which marketing efforts generate the best leads.
