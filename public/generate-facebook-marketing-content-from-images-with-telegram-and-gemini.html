What This Workflow Does

This automation solves the challenge of creating consistent, engaging Facebook content from visual assets. Many businesses struggle to maintain an active social media presence because creating posts takes too much time - capturing images, writing captions, selecting hashtags, and formatting everything properly.

The workflow eliminates these bottlenecks by automatically transforming Telegram image messages into polished Facebook posts. When you or your team sends a photo via Telegram, the system uses Gemini AI to analyze the image and generate compelling marketing content complete with captions, hashtags, and post formatting.

How It Works

1. Image Submission via Telegram

Team members send images through a designated Telegram channel or bot. They can include optional voice notes or text captions to provide context about the image.

2. AI Content Generation

The workflow sends the image to Gemini AI, which analyzes visual elements and generates multiple caption options, relevant hashtags, and a post description optimized for engagement.

3. Content Review & Approval

The generated content gets sent back to a Telegram approval channel where managers can quickly review and approve posts with one click or request revisions.

4. Facebook Post Creation

Approved posts are automatically formatted and scheduled on your Facebook page according to your preferred posting schedule and content guidelines.
